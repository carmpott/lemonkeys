Hello!

Hello, there. My name is Carmela Potter. I am Filipina American. I spent the first 18 years of my life in southern California. I loved where I grew up but I had this gut feeling that I needed to see and do more. I moved to Washingtion State in 2016 to figure out what I wanted to do with my life. I first enrolled at Highline Community College still not sure about my future. I ended up only being there for about 2 quarters and then I dropped out. Shortly after, I met my husband who is currently serving the United States Army. Being a military spouse has taught me to be strong and to not give up so easily. I started to realize that I did not want to give up on myself. So I made the descision to go back to school.

Today, I am working at an Amazon hazmat fulfillment center in Dupont, WA. I decided to go back to school through amazon's career choice program. My goals right now are to obtain my associates degree and then transfer to UW Tacoma.
